Glossary (terms as I understand them):

  • CC: CreativeClash, an art gifting game. Operates on a discord server and website (xcreativeclashx.net)
  • Aethy: Aethy.com, a mastodon instance
  • DreamWidth: Blogging website DreamWidth.org
  • Squidge: Offers a variety of services but the relevant service is "fandom" image hosting
  • Para Pride: A way for paraphiles to celebrate their continued existence in a world that doesn't understand them at best and wants them dead at worst. May be conveyed with flags and symbols, talking in positive terms about their experience, or posting fantasies.
  • MAP: Minor Attracted Person. Someone who experiences sexual and/or romantic attraction to people under the age of consent.
  • Pro-contact (pro-c): those who believe that people under the age of consent ARE able to give informed consent to romantic/sexual contact with adults (specifics of the belief vary from person to person)
  • Anti-contact (anti-c): those who believe that people under the age of consent ARE NOT able to give informed consent to romantic/sexual contact with adults (specifics of the belief vary from person to person)
  • Contact Discourse: Arguing the ethics of a pro-c vs anti-c stance. A very complex mostly intracommunity topic that outsiders don't have a very nuanced view on.

You don't have to agree with a contact stance, but by these definitions neither side thinks they're abusing anyone. Also, keep in mind "minor" is an enormous age range from newborn to somewhere between 14-18. This nuance is completely flattened in a simplistic "pro-c vs anti-c" argument.

https://aethy.com/@staff/110681799980955971 The initial post appears to have been deleted, and I didn't screenshot it. Oh well.
